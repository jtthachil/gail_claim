Why This Matters

Psychological injuries are real, compensable, and more common than you think

Common Psychological Injuries

  • 🧠
    Anxiety Disorders

    Persistent worry, panic attacks, social anxiety related to work situations

  • 😔
    Depression

    Persistent sadness, loss of interest, feelings of hopelessness about work

  • 💥
    PTSD

    From workplace trauma, bullying, or witnessing distressing events

  • 😵
    Burnout Syndrome

    Emotional exhaustion, cynicism, reduced sense of accomplishment

Common Workplace Causes

  • 👥
    Workplace Bullying

    Harassment, intimidation, or systematic mistreatment by colleagues or supervisors

  • 📈
    Excessive Workload

    Unrealistic deadlines, impossible targets, chronic understaffing

  • ❓
    Role Ambiguity

    Unclear expectations, conflicting instructions, lack of proper training

  • 🚫
    Lack of Support

    Dismissive management, ignored complaints, inadequate resources
