Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and pricing

Is this legal advice?

No. This is structured support. We do not offer legal or clinical guidance. We provide practical guidance and support to help you navigate the claims process, but we are not lawyers or medical professionals. For legal advice, we can refer you to qualified legal practitioners.

Can I claim if I haven't been bullied?

Yes. You can claim if your workplace failed its duty of care‚Äîeven without bullying. Psychological injuries can result from various workplace factors including excessive workload, poor management, lack of support, unsafe work environment, or organizational changes. Bullying is just one of many potential causes.

What happens after the quiz?

You'll be invited to book a free intake session. During this 30-minute call, we'll discuss your specific situation in detail, answer your questions, and recommend the most appropriate service level for your needs. There's no obligation to purchase anything.

How long does a claim take?

Each case varies. We'll walk you through the process step by step. Simple claims may take 3-6 months, while more complex cases can take 12-18 months or longer. The timeline depends on factors like medical evidence, workplace cooperation, and claim complexity. We'll give you realistic expectations based on your specific situation.

What's the difference between the Claim Packs?

Pack A provides essential guidance for understanding your situation. Pack B adds comprehensive preparation tools and a clarification call. Pack C includes personalized strategy, document review, and ongoing support.

How is App Navigator pricing determined?

Navigator pricing depends on case complexity, timeline, and level of support needed. We provide a free consultation to assess your needs and provide a transparent quote.

Do you guarantee claim success?

We cannot guarantee outcomes as every case is unique. However, we provide the tools, guidance, and support to give you the best possible chance of success.

Can I upgrade from one service to another?

Yes! If you start with a Claim Pack and need more support, we can upgrade you to an App Session or Navigator service. Previous payments are credited toward the upgrade.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets through our secure Stripe payment system. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee on all digital products. If you're not satisfied, we'll provide a full refund. Service-based products have specific refund terms outlined in our policy.
